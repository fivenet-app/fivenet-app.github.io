## Features

- Searching citizens by their name, date of birth, phone number, traffic infraction points and open fines.
- Setting citizen "properties"
  - Wanted state
  - Traffic infraction points
  - Mug Shot
  - Attributes (e.g., person is dangerous, violent)
  - And more...
