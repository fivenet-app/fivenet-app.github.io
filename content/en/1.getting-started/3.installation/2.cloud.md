FiveNet Cloud is available today as a managed hosting option for communities that want us to run and maintain FiveNet for them.

The service is hosted by ZerBytes UG (haftungsbeschrankt).

### Why Choose FiveNet Cloud

- **Managed Hosting**: We run and maintain your FiveNet instance for you.
- **Less Operational Overhead**: You do not need to build and maintain your own Docker or Kubernetes setup for FiveNet.
- **Ongoing Updates**: We keep your FiveNet instance up to date as new releases become available.
- **Direct Support**: Cloud customers can reach out through Discord support tickets for onboarding and operational questions.

### What You Still Need To Provide

- Access to the gameserver database that FiveNet should use.
- The required plugin integration on your gameserver.
- Any server-specific configuration needed for your community and framework.

FiveNet Cloud reduces the infrastructure work on your side, but you still need to connect it to your gameserver environment.

### Getting Started

1. Join the FiveNet Discord.
2. Create a support ticket for FiveNet Cloud.
3. Share the details needed for your setup, such as your community, gameserver environment, and database connectivity.
4. We will guide you through onboarding and the remaining setup steps.

### FAQs

::card-group{class="sm:grid-cols-1"}
::card
---
title: What happens to my data?
icon: i-mdi-database
---
Your gameserver database must be accessible to FiveNet. If you later switch to a self-hosted instance, you can request the attachment data, such as avatars.
::
::card
---
title: Is there a free tier?
icon: i-mdi-currency-usd-off
---
Currently, there is no free tier available for FiveNet.
::
::card
---
title: How do I migrate from self-hosted to cloud?
icon: i-mdi-cloud-upload
---
No full migration is required. Since your FiveNet data, such as citizens and documents, remains tied to your database, the main extra step is handling attachment data if needed.
::
::card
---
title: How do I migrate from cloud to self-hosted?
icon: i-mdi-cloud-download
---
Since FiveNet data, such as citizens and documents, is stored in your database, you mainly need a copy of the attachment data for your self-hosted instance. The FiveNet Cloud team can assist with this process.
::
::card
---
title: Do I still need technical setup on my side?
icon: i-mdi-tools
---
Yes. FiveNet Cloud removes most of the infrastructure work, but you still need to provide database access and complete the required plugin integration for your gameserver.
::
::
