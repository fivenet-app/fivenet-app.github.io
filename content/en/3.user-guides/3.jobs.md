## Overview: MOTD and Radio Frequency

Use the quick-connect button on the in-game tablet to join the configured radio frequency.

![Feature Jobs - Overview](/images/screenshots/features-jobs.webp)

## Colleagues

View colleagues and open their profiles.

![Feature Jobs - Colleague Profile](/images/screenshots/features-jobs-colleague.webp)

## Colleague Activity

Track colleague activity, such as joining or leaving the job, promotions, demotions, and other updates.

![Feature Jobs - Activity](/images/screenshots/features-jobs-activity.webp)

## Timeclock

Use the timeclock to track on-duty hours. This can also be extended via the game server plugin for jobs without built-in on/off duty support.

![Feature Jobs - Timeclock](/images/screenshots/features-jobs-timeclock.webp)

## Conduct Register

Record positive and negative employee conduct in the conduct register.

![Feature Jobs - Conduct Register](/images/screenshots/features-jobs-conductregister.webp)
