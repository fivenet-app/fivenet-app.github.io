## Features

- View the live positions of your colleagues (visibility depends on your faction's permissions).
- Add map markers using different types:
  - **Dots**: Simple point markers.
  - **Circles**: Highlight areas of interest.
  - **Icons**: Represent specific items or locations. You can browse available icons in [Material Design Icons by Pictogrammers](https://pictogrammers.com/library/mdi/).
