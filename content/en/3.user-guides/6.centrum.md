The **Dispatch Center** provides a central place to coordinate dispatches and units.

## Key Features

- **Automated or manual dispatching**: Assign dispatches based on your workflow.
- **Real-time activity feed**: Monitor dispatch and unit activity as it happens.
- **Unit management**: Create, update, and manage units.

## Benefits

- Improves coordination and helps reduce response times.
- Increases operational transparency through live activity tracking.
- Simplifies resource planning with clearer unit status.
